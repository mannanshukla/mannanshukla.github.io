Learning Roadmap

I. General Reading

Read/watch these quick, non-intensive articles/videos about the basics of economics, cryptocurrency, etc. You will find the information to be eventually redundant, but that is the goal. You should understand all of the general concepts, and be able to swiftly skim through similar resources.

  • An Overview of Economics - University of Washington, Seattle
  • Economics Basics: Supply & Demand - University of Kentucky
  • A Short Introduction to the World of Cryptocurrencies - Federal Reserve Bank of St. Louis
  • Cryptocurrencies: A Guide to Getting Started - World Economic Forum Community Paper
  • Essential Guide to Cryptocurrency and Bitcoin - Foundation for Economic Education
  • Investing in Cryptocurrency - Morgan Stanley Wealth Management Global Investment Office
  • How to Trade Bitcoin - FOREX.com educational guide
  • Bitcoin Starter Guide - Bitcoin Basics and Benefits

Long-term Reading (Optional)
